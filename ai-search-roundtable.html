About the Event

AI search engines like ChatGPT and Perplexity now influence how customers find businesses. Many businesses still rely only on traditional SEO. As a result, AI tools often show missing or incorrect information.

This roundtable explains how AI systems collect brand data and the simple steps you can take to improve accuracy.

You Will Learn

  • How AI search engines build knowledge
  • The most common visibility gaps
  • Why details are sometimes wrong or missing
  • Ways to prepare your business for AI-driven discovery

The session includes real examples and practical advice you can use right away.
