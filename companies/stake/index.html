Overview

Stake is an Australian-based online brokerage platform that provides commission-free access to US and Australian stock markets. Founded with a mission to democratize investing, Stake enables retail investors to trade stocks and ETFs without paying brokerage fees on US trades.

The platform is particularly popular among younger, tech-savvy investors seeking exposure to international markets, especially US tech stocks. Stake offers a mobile-first trading experience with real-time market data, fractional share trading, and educational resources.

Stake operates under Australian Financial Services License (AFSL) regulation and partners with licensed clearing houses to execute trades securely. The platform has grown significantly since launch, attracting hundreds of thousands of users across Australia and expanding internationally.

Funds & Custody

AUD Funds: Held on trust at NAB via Stakeshop AFSL Pty Ltd in a client monies account per Corporations Act s981B. All client funds are held in segregated trust accounts, separate from Stake's operational funds.

FX and Payments: Facilitated through Airwallex Pty Ltd (AFSL 487221) providing non-cash payment services, foreign exchange services (0.55% fee, min $2), and remittance services. Airwallex provides Virtual Wallet with unique BSB/account number for each customer.

US Custody: DriveWealth LLC holds US securities via DTC (Depository Trust Company). Uninvested USD cash is swept into omnibus bank deposit account at Citibank, held by DriveWealth for customer benefit. FDIC insured up to USD $250,000 per customer.

Compliance: Professional Indemnity insurance held per ASIC RG 126. Clients receive PDS/TMD documents before acquiring any financial product. Dispute resolution available through AFCA (1800 931 678).
