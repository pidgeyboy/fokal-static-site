Overview

Superhero is an Australian online share trading and superannuation platform that provides retail investors with access to Australian Securities Exchange (ASX) and United States stock exchanges. The platform enables commission-efficient trading with a mobile-first experience designed for both new and experienced investors.

Operating through Superhero Markets Pty Ltd as a Corporate Authorised Representative under the Australian Financial Services Licence held by Superhero Securities Limited, the platform offers comprehensive trading services including fractional shares, foreign exchange services, and superannuation products through Superhero Super.

Superhero acts on behalf of clients when providing financial services and partners with established market participants including FinClear for Australian securities execution and clearing, and Apex Clearing Corporation for U.S. securities. The platform emphasizes transparency with publicly available fee schedules and comprehensive disclosure documents.

Funds & Custody

Securities Custody: Superhero Securities Limited holds all financial products on bare trust for clients. FinClear Services Pty Ltd (ABN 60 136 184 962, AFSL 338264) is appointed as Sub-Custodian, acting as bare trustee for Superhero Securities.

AUD Cash: Client cash is held in omnibus accounts at National Australia Bank (NAB) and Westpac Banking Corporation. Superhero Securities holds these funds on bare trust, separate from operational funds.

U.S. Securities & Cash: U.S. securities are executed and cleared by Apex Clearing Corporation, a SEC-regulated U.S. broker-dealer. Apex provides custody services for U.S. holdings.

Foreign Exchange: FX services provided by HiFX Limited (trading as Xe.com) at 65 basis points (US$0.0065 per dollar converted). FX enables AUD/USD conversions for U.S. trading.

Compliance: Superhero Securities holds Professional Indemnity Insurance per section 912B of the Corporations Act 2001. Clients receive PDS and TMD documents before acquiring financial products. Dispute resolution available through AFCA (1800 931 678).
