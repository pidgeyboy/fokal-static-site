Making brands visible in the AI era

We're solving the visibility crisis that's costing brands millions in lost revenue—because great products shouldn't disappear where customers look.

The Problem

The game changed, but nobody was keeping score

In late 2023, I noticed something strange. Brands with incredible SEO—#1 rankings, strong content, healthy traffic—were completely invisible in ChatGPT and Perplexity.

I tested 100 customer queries for a client. Their brand appeared in 8 of them. Eight out of 100.

AI platforms were choosing which brands to recommend, and most brands had no tools to measure or fix it.

Our Mission

Make visibility measurable, fixable, and fair

Every day, millions of customers ask AI platforms questions that drive purchase decisions. AI gives them answers, recommends brands, and decides who gets seen.

We built Fokal to give brands visibility into this new layer—across AI platforms, media coverage, comparison engines, and every other place customers actually look.

How We Work

Built on principles that matter

Every decision we make is guided by these core values.

Transparency First

No black boxes. Every gap comes with the exact query, platform, and reason. You understand every problem we find.

Data Over Opinions

We test thousands of real queries and track actual appearances. When we say you have a gap, it's because we found it in the wild.

Built for Action

Insights without action are worthless. Every gap comes with specific, prioritized fixes you can implement today.
